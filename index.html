Isn't that the entire aim of a rifle to keep you from going too close? You'll want to identify your target and fire as soon as possible, especially if you have a lot of foes to contend with. Allowing them to come too near will rapidly overwhelm and destroy you! You can keep your adversaries to the edge of the screen if you're fast enough on the draw.
